Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I am learning to buy properties and need help finding and evaluating investment properties. Can you help?


A: Absolutely! We can evaluate your deals and educate you on how we evaluate ours. We can assist with rehabs, wholesales or rentals. Whether you are selling a house to us or buying a house from us, we can help. We promise to provide helpful information and various solutions to your situation.


Q: Do you buy houses fast from Investors?


A: Yes we do. Call our office at 844-I-Luv-to-Invest anytime you have a property to sell. You can wholesale your properties to us or, if we can't buy it, we can help you get it sold. We also take assignments.


Q: Do you only sell houses or single family homes?


A: No. We sell many types of properties including houses, duplexes, apartment buildings, condominiums, town houses, land, and commercial real estate. However, our primary focus is single-family residential homes.


Q: Where do you sell or rent houses?


A: We sells houses in Salt Lake and the outlying area. We invest in and frequently have inventory in the following cities: Provo, Riverton, Salt Lake, West Valley, Sugar House, Draper, Daybreak, and Price, Utah.


Q: Why Buy From ILuv2Invest?


A: ILuv2Invest sells houses, many of which are perfect for the first-time home buyer. We have been expertly trained to spot those diamond-in-the-rough properties, houses which are loaded with potential. We buy these properties below market value, then work with professional contractors to rehab them into lovely homes that remain surprisingly affordable.

Frequently Asked Questions About Selling Property

Q. What if my house is already listed with an agent?


A. You can still sell your house to us, however, you may need to compensate your agent according to the terms of your agreement.


Q. Can I still sell my house if I'm in the process of evicting a tenant or have tenant troubles?


A. Yes. We can close escrow with the tenant occupying the property.


Q. Can I sell my house in probate?


A. Yes. We will make a valid offer subject to the completion of the probate.


Q. I need to sell my inherited home. Will you buy it?


A. Yes. We will make you an offer. If you agree, we can close and you will receive your money from escrow in as little as 7 days!


Q. How long does it take to get an offer?


A. Although every situation is unique, it is not unusual to get an offer within 48 hours.


Q. Will you list my house?


A. If that is your best option we can help you to do that. However, if you want to sell your house fast and avoid commissions, in many cases we will just pay cash for your home. This eliminates all agent commissions, home inspections, appraisals, and bank loan approvals.


Q. Am I obligated to take your offer?


A. Not at all.


Q. Do you buy houses or properties in need of repair?


A. We will purchase your home whether or not it needs repairs or other work.


Q. What types of property do you buy?


A. We buy any type of property in any condition. This includes single family homes, multi-family homes, apartments, and commercial properties.


Q. What do you mean by a "cash offer?"


A. We use our own cash to buy your property, which eliminates the need for a loan or any approval from outside sources. We have the power to make decisions and close quickly.
